About Me

I am pursuing a Master's in Nanoengineering at North Carolina A&T State University, with a strong foundation in computer science and a growing passion for physics and computational research. As a Graduate Research Assistant at the Joint School of Nanoscience and Nanoengineering (JSNN), I work at the intersection of nanotechnology and computational approaches.

With several years of hands-on experience as a software engineer and entrepreneur, I bring a unique perspective to research. I founded Cdevi Technology in Nepal and served as CTO at Technology Spot, combining technical leadership with innovative problem-solving. I'm eager to collaborate with universities, institutions, and companies on projects that create real impact through the fusion of nanotechnology and computation.

Beyond research and development, I have experience as a lecturer at two institutions in Nepal, where I shared my knowledge and mentored the next generation of technologists. My diverse background spans leadership roles in community service organizations and academic excellence recognized through multiple scholarships and awards.
